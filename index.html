      Archetype (choose one):
      • Technician – Precise, disciplined, and detail-oriented. Wins through clean execution and positional mastery.
      • Scrambler – Creative, flexible, and unorthodox. Thrives in chaos and transitional positions.
      • Pummeler – Physical, relentless, and hand-fight heavy. Controls ties and wears opponents down.
      • Counter-wrestler – Patient, reactive, and opportunistic. Excels at re-attacks and defensive scoring.
      • Chain-wrestler – Constant motion, seamless transitions, and layered attacks. Overwhelms opponents with pace.
      Body Type (choose one):
      • Compact/Powerful – Strong hips, explosive movements, excels in short-offense and upper-body ties.
      • Long/Lanky – Uses reach effectively, strong in rides, cradles, and leverage-based attacks.
      • Balanced/Athletic – Versatile mover with no extreme strengths or limitations; adaptable to many styles.
